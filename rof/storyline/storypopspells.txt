Planes of Power Spell Guide
<br><br>
Most spells in the Planes of Power expansion can be aquired by turning in <c "#ffd700">Ethereal Parchments</c> for level 61 and 62 spells, <c "#ffd700">Spectral Parchments</c> for level 63 and 64 spells, or <c "#ffd700">Glyphed Rune Words</c> for level 65 spells.<br> <br><br>

<c "#35db24">Spell Turn In NPCs</c>
In the Plane of Knowledge Library located in the center of the zone are several Guild Master type NPCs that you can exchange parchments and glyph words to for a RANDOM spell. There are no level or class restrictions on who can exchange the items. IE, a warrior can still turn in parchments to get a wizard spell.<br><br>

Bard - <c "#dc7633">Minstrel Eoweril</c><br>
Beastlord - <c "#dc7633">Primalist Saosith</c><br>
Cleric - <c "#dc7633">Vicar Ceraen</c><br>
Druid - <c "#dc7633">Wanderer Astobin</c><br>
Enchanter - <c "#dc7633">Illusionist Jerup</c><br>
Magician - <c "#dc7633">Elementalist Somat</c><br>
Necromancer - <c "#dc7633">Heretic Drahur</c><br>
Paladin - <c "#dc7633">Cavalier Waut</c><br>
Ranger - <c "#dc7633">Pathfinder Viliken</c><br>
Shadowknight - <c "#dc7633">Reaver Nydlil</c><br>
Shaman - <c "#dc7633">Mystic Abomin</c><br>
Wizard - <c "#dc7633">Channeler Olaemos</c><br>
