<c "#ff0000">PLANE OF STORMS</c>
<br><br>
In the 'ocean' or 'Hleyta' giant camp, kill for a Storm Giant Head & a Storm Volaas Beard. Also, kill Faruek the Bold for an Esoteric Medallion.
<br><br>
In the 'desert' or'Jotna' Giants giant camp, kill for a Storm Taarid Bone.
<br><br>
In the 'forest' ot 'Irmin' giants camp, kill for a Storm Satuur Sash. Also, kill Laruken the Rigid, Paruek the Strong (or Zertuken the Unyielding) for an Esoteric Medallion.
<br><br>
Head into the cave at the zone in and go to the last area to find Askr the Lost. Hail him and then give him the Storm Giant Head (you get it back). Then, follow along with Askr the Lost to get Askr's Bag of Verity.
<br><br>
Combine the Storm Taarid Bone, the Storm Volaas Beard and the Storm Satuur Sash in Askr's Bag of Verity to make Askr's Sealed Bag of Verity.
<br><br>
Give Askr the Sealed Bag of Verity and say, "What Bastion of Thunder?" and he will give you another Askr's Bag of Verity.
<br><br>
Combine the 2 Esoteric Medallion in Askr's Bag of Verityto make an Esoteric Meld,
<br><br>
Now head into the frog / tornado area and click on the small shrine in the middle, to zone into the Torden, Bastion of Thunder and complete your flag.
<br><br>
<c "#35db24"> Post-Flag - Plane of Knowledge</c><br><br>
Now go talk to Seer Mal Nae`Shi in the Plane of Knowledge, sit down and say to her, "Unlock my memories." 
<br><br>
Now talk to Gram Dunnar in that same building and say, 'I will tell you stories.' to wrap up this leg of the progression and get a possible upgrade to your Intricate Wooden Totem and / or AA(s) (you don't get these everytime).
