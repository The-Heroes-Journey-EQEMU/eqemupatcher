<c "#ff0000">PLANE OF STORMS</c>
<br><br>
In the south part of the zone kill <c "#ff0000">Hleyta</c> giants at the camp, and loot a <c "#ffd700">Storm Giant Head</c>, and a <c "#ffd700">Storm Volaas Beard</c>. Also, kill <c "#ff0000">Faruek the Bold</c> for an <c "#ffd700">Esoteric Medallion</c>.
<br><br>
In the west par of the zone kill <c "#ff0000">Jotna</c> giants at the camp, and loot a <c "#ffd700">Storm Taarid Bone</c>.
<br><br>
In the north part of the zone kill <c "#ff0000">Irmin</c> giants at the camp, and loot a <c "#ffd700">Storm Satuur Sash</c>. Also, kill <c "#ff0000">Laruken the Rigid, Paruek the Strong,</c> or <c "#ff0000">Zertuken the Unyielding</c> for an <c "#ffd700">Esoteric Medallion</c>.
<br><br>
Head into the cave at the zone in and go to the last area to find <c "#dc7633">Askr the Lost</c>. <c "#00ff00">Hail</c> him, and then give him the <c "#ffd700">Storm Giant Head</c>. Then, tell <c "#dc7633">Askr the Lost</c> '<c "#00ff00">continue</c>', follow the dialog, and select '<c "#00ff00">continue</c>' to get <c "#ffd700">Askr's Bag of Verity</c>.
<br><br>
Combine the <c "#ffd700">Storm Taarid Bone</c>, the <c "#ffd700">Storm Volaas Beard</c>, and the <c "#ffd700">Storm Satuur Sash</c> in <c "#ffd700">Askr's Bag of Verity</c> to make <c "#ffd700">Askr's Sealed Bag of Verity</c>.
<br><br>
Give <c "#dc7633">Askr the Lost</c> the <c "#ffd700">Sealed Bag of Verity</c> and follow the dialog, You will receive another <c "#ffd700">Askr's Bag of Verity</c>.
<br><br>
Combine the 2 <c "#ffd700">Esoteric Medallion</c>(different giants) in <c "#ffd700">Askr's Bag of Verity</c> to make an <c "#ffd700">Esoteric Meld</c>
<br><br>
Hand the <c "#ffd700">Esoteric Meld</c> to <c "#dc7633">Ask the Lost</c>.<br>
<c "#dada99">You receive a character flag!</c><br><br>
Now head into the frog / tornado area and click on the small shrine in the middle, to zone into the <c "#35db24">Torden, Bastion of Thunder</c>.<br>
<c "#dada99">You receive a character flag!</c><br><br>
