<c "#ff0000">DRUNDER, THE FORTRESS OF ZEK (PLANE OF TACTICS)</c>
<br><br>
<c "#35db24"> Part 1 - Tallon Zek</c><br><br>
Locate <c "#ff0000">Tallon Zek</c> and kill him, upon his death.<br>
<c "#dada99">You receive a character flag!</c><br><br>
A Planar Projection will spawn, no need to Hail him.<br>
You can <c "#00ff00">Click</c> the wall where <c "#ff0000">Tallon Zek</c> was originally at to zone out.<br><br>


<c "#35db24"> Part 2 - Vallon Zek</c><br><br>
Locate <c "#ff0000">Vallon Zek</c>, opposite way that <c "#ff0000">Tallon Zek</c> was. After you kill <c "#ff0000">Vallon Zek</c>.<br>
<c "#dada99">You receive a character flag!</c><br><br>
A Planar Projection will spawn, no need to Hail him.<br>
You can zone out by clicking the wall where <c "#ff0000">Vallon Zek</c> was originally standing (panel on wall).
<br><br>

<c "#35db24"> Part 3 - Rallos Zek the Warlord</c><br><br>
Head into Drunder, Fortress of Zek. Head up the steps on the far side of the pit / arena and go up the steps there.<br>You are after <c "#ff0000">Decorin Berik</c> and <c "#ff0000">Decorin Grunhork</c>.<br>They will need to be killed within a very short time of each other.<br><br>
Once they are dead, this will spawn <c "#ff0000">Tallon Zek</c>. This fight is identical to his fight above.
<br><br>
Once he is dead, <c "#ff0000">Vallon Zek</c> will spawn. This fight is identical to his fight above.
<br><br>
Once done, go up the steps to the north to engage <c "#ff0000">Rallos Zek the Warlord</c>. He will despawn (port away) at <c "#ff0000">50%</c> and reappear in the pit / arena. <br><br>
After he despawns, hurry down to the pit / area as you are now on a timer (he will despawn after 30 minutes, even if you are actively in battle with him). Finish off <c "#ff0000">Rallos Zek the Warlord</c>. Upon his death.<br>
<c "#dada99">You receive a character flag!</c><br><br>
A Planar Projection will spawn, no need to Hail him.<br><br>


<c "#35db24"> Post-Flag - Plane of Knowledge</c><br><br>
Now talk to <c "#dc7633">Gram Dunnar</c>, <c "#00ff00">Hail</c> him, and follow his dialog [<c "#00ff00">stories</c>].
