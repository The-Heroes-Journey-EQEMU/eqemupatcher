<c "#ff0000">TOWER OF SOLUSEK RO</c>
<br><br>


<c "#35db24">Zone Information</c><br><br>
This zone is fairly straight forward. There are 5 wings that each contain a boss at the end. After you kill each mini boss and get your flag there will be a firepot that teleports you to the base of each wing.<br><br>


<c "#35db24"> Part One - Mini Boss</c><br><br>
Kill the following mini bosses:<br><c "#ff0000">Xuzl<br>
<c "#dada99">You receive a character flag!</c><br><br>
Arlyxir<br>
<c "#dada99">You receive a character flag!</c><br><br>
Jiva<br>
<c "#dada99">You receive a character flag!</c><br><br>
Rizlona<br>
<c "#dada99">You receive a character flag!</c><br><br>
The Protector of Dresolik</c><br>
<c "#dada99">You receive a character flag!</c><br><br>
A Planar Projection will spawn after each death, you don't need to Hail them.<br><br>
<c "#35db24"> Part Two - Solusek Ro</c><br><br>
Click the port up in the middle of the main room to get to <c "#ff0000">Solusek Ro</c>'s area.<br>
Kill <c "#ff0000">Solusek Ro</c><br>
<c "#dada99">You receive a character flag!</c><br><br>

A Planar Projection</c> will spawn, you don't have to Hail it.<br><br>
Open the door on the floor, and jump into the <c "#35db24">Lava Pit</c>, and swim down into <c "#35db24">Plane of Fire</c>!<br><br>
You know have access to the <c "#35db24">Elemental Planes!</c><br><br>
 
<c "#35db24"> Post-Flag - Plane of Knowledge</c><br><br>
Now talk to <c "#dc7633">Gram Dunnar</c>, <c "#00ff00">Hail</c> him, and follow his dialog [<c "#00ff00">stories</c>].
