<c "#ffd700">Progression Overview</c><br>------------<br>
<c "#35db24">Progression 1 - Classic EverQuest:</c><br>

The foundation of the game—featuring Antonica, Faydwer, and Odus, and the legendary starting cities and dungeons that shaped the world.<br>

• Over 80 zones including Nagafen’s Lair, Guk, and Permafrost.<br>
• Classic epic quests, faction-based conflicts, and the original <c "#dc7633">max level cap of 50.</c><br>
• The beginnings of multiclassing and personal progression in The Heroes’ Journey.<br>
<br><br>
<c "#35db24">Progression 2 - The Ruins of Kunark:</c><br>

The first expansion, introducing the vast continent of Kunark, new zones, and the powerful Iksar race.<br>

• Over 25 new zones, including Sebilis, Chardok, and Karnor’s Castle.<br>
• <c "#dc7633">Level cap raised to 60</c> with new class-defining abilities.<br>
• The introduction of high-tier raid encounters, including Trakanon and Venril Sathir.<br>
<br><br>
<c "#35db24">Progression 3 - The Scars of Velious:</c><br>

A land of frozen wastelands, giants, and dragons, Velious brought larger-scale raids and faction wars.<br>

• The faction struggle between Coldain, Claws of Veeshan, and Kromzek.<br>
• New high-level raids: Temple of Veeshan, Sleeper’s Tomb, and Kael Drakkel.<br>
• Armor quests and new raid progression tiers.<br>
<br><br>
<c "#35db24">Progression 4 - The Shadows of Luclin:</c><br>

The moon of Norrath, bringing alien landscapes, new technologies, and the Vah Shir race.<br>

• New zones including The Nexus, Akheva Ruins, and Vex Thal.<br>
• Alternate Advancement (AA) system introduced, expanding character progression.<br>
• Mounts, Bazaar trading, and the Plane of Shadows.<br>
<c "#dc7633">Level cap increased to 65.</c><br>
<br><br>
<c "#35db24">Progression 5 - Planes of Power:</c><br>

The Planes of the Gods, introducing high-level raid content, planar progression, and a hub-based teleportation system.<br>

• New planar zones: Plane of Time, Plane of Valor, and Plane of Innovation.<br>
• Raid encounters against powerful deities.<br>
• The Plane of Knowledge, serving as a central hub for players.<br>
