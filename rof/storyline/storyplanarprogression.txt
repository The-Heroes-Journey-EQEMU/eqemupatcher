THE HERO'S JOURNEY PLANAR PROGRESSION GUIDE
<br><br>
Following this guide should allow you to quickly move thru the mostly linear Planes of Power Progression. While there are several ways to progress, this is the most direct path with the least amount of "fixing flags".
<br><br>
If you do end up with any out of order flags, you can use this guide: <a href="file:///storyline/PlanarProgression/storyflagfixplanprog.txt">Fixing Your PoP Flags</a>
<br><br>
<c "#35db24">TIER 0 - Pre Work - Plane of Knowledge</c>
<br><br>
Locate Gram Dunnar in Plane of Knowledge and say "craft" to receive <c "#ffd700">Intricate Wooden Figurine</c> - You will receive one for each of your classes.<br>
        Return to Gram after progressing thru a flag to receive AAXP and stat increases to your Figurine
